Welcome to SAdjHUB, Your Ultimate Plug for All Things Amapiano! Step into the rhythm and feel the vibe — SAdjHUB is your go-to destination for everything about South Africa’s hottest Amapiano artists: Babalwa, Mellow & Sleazy, and Scotts Maphuma. From behind-the-beat stories to the latest drops, we’ve got the juice, the grooves, and the culture — all in one spot. Whether you're a day-one fan or just discovering the sound, we’re here to keep your playlist fresh and your heart in sync with the beat. Dive in, explore, and vibe out.

Babalwa M

The soulful voice of Amapiano, Babalwa is all about elegance, emotion, and ethereal vibes. With her dreamy vocals and magnetic presence, she brings a touch of magic to every track she graces. Whether she’s floating over deep log drums or harmonizing with legends, Babalwa is the sound of beauty in the Amapiano wave.

Scotts Maphuma

A rising star in the Amapiano universe, Scotts Maphuma brings smooth, soulful vibes with every track. His unique sound blends deep grooves, mellow keys, and rich rhythms that hit right in the feels. If you're into Amapiano with heart and depth — Scotts is your guy.

Mellow & Sleazy

The dynamic duo shaking up the Amapiano scene! Known for their bass-heavy sound and infectious energy, Mellow & Sleazy are the beatmakers behind some of the biggest anthems in South Africa. From viral hits to club bangers, they’re not just making music — they’re creating a whole vibe.

Prince Kaybee

Hitmaker. Producer. Powerhouse. Prince Kaybee is one of South Africa’s most consistent chart-toppers, known for iconic tracks like "Charlotte", "Gugulethu", and "Fetch Your Life." His music blends soulful house with high-energy beats that keep dance floors alive and emotions running deep. With every release, he proves he’s not just making hits — he’s shaping the sound of a generation.
