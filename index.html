Babalwa M

                                       

The soulful voice of Amapiano, Babalwa is all about elegance, emotion, and ethereal vibes. With her dreamy vocals and magnetic presence, she brings a touch of magic to every track she graces. Whether she’s floating over deep log drums or harmonizing with legends, Babalwa is the sound of beauty in the Amapiano wave.

               
               
                   

Scotts Maphuma

                                       

A rising star in the Amapiano universe, Scotts Maphuma brings smooth, soulful vibes with every track. His unique sound blends deep grooves, mellow keys, and rich rhythms that hit right in the feels. If you're into Amapiano with heart and depth — Scotts is your guy.

               
               
                   

Mellow & Sleazy

                                       

The dynamic duo shaking up the Amapiano scene! Known for their bass-heavy sound and infectious energy, Mellow & Sleazy are the beatmakers behind some of the biggest anthems in South Africa. From viral hits to club bangers, they’re not just making music — they’re creating a whole vibe.
